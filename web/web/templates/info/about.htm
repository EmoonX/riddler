About

Riddler is a three-sided application consisting of
browser extension, website and Discord bot.

How does it work?

  1. You install the extension for your favorite browser;
  2. You log in to the system using Discord credentials (no need for emails or awkwardly uploading avatars);
  3. Progress is saved on every new level and page visited, without the need for bookmarks;
  4. Points are awarded upon solving levels and unlocking achievements;
  5. You end up competing in leaderboards, meeting new people and being part of a whole riddle-solving ecosystem.

How to use the extension/system?

  1. First of all, install the extension officially for your favorite web browser:
  2. Open the login page, be it by extension button, navbar button, or by clicking here.
  3. Authorize Riddler's application using your Discord credentials (by creating a Discord account first if you don't have one):
  4. Select your country among the listed ones and click the Create account button:
  5. Your account is now created. PSA: You need to be part of at least one Discord server the bot is in to enable it to interact with you; and vice-versa. Be sure to join Riddler's Wonderland II for up-to-date Riddler announcements, updates and discussion.
  6. And that's it! Now go play your favorite riddles and enjoy all the goodnesses Riddler has to offer.
