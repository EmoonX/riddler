Terms & Conditions

Regarding online riddles

Riddler is built upon the content of online riddles, hosted on third-party domains by their own creators. Such content primarily includes name, logo, levels' images (small) thumbnails, and unlocked pages (URLs).

While the former three may be or not be copyrighted material, their usage falls under the fair use doctrine, by common sense. As such, the app does not legally or pragmatically require prior permission or awareness from said content owners.

As for URLs, no recorded pages are openly visible until given player reaches them on the external riddle domain itself. Therefore, no spoilery content exists publicly on riddler.app, ensuring the secrecy of all riddles' content and fair game to everyone.

Regarding players

Same golden rule as overall riddle play: you shall not openly share levels and page URLs (e.g in the form of a public walkthrough).

Overly troll-ish behavior is also heavenly frowned upon, such as: intentionally rating every riddle level 1 heart; impersonating someone else, spamming on Wonderland (outside #spam, of course), etc.

Riddler admins reserve the right to warn and act upon aforementioned behavior.

By using Riddler, extension and website — and the riddles supported by it — you hereby acknowledge and consent to the aforementioned Terms and Conditions.
